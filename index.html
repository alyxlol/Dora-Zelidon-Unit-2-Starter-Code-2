LGBTQ+ RIGHTS

LGBTQ+ rights have often been overlooked. People part of the community have been treated as lower, and as if they don't deserve as much rights as anyone else. This is a really big problem in our community because no one should be discriminated based on their sexuality. In order for people to be more aware of this, below are some resources where you can learn about your rights, and where you can find additional support.

Global Human Rights

This website works to fight for equality by providing resources for legal aid, activism, health, and safety access. They support and protect many different communities as well, not just the LGBTQ!

LGBTQ Rights-Know Your Rights

This website here will help you become more aware of your LGBTQ rights, and how they may be protected in real life situations
